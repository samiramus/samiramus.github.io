Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is this app?

This app is a workout notebook designed to help you track your sets, reps, and weights for various exercises. It includes a database of common exercises, timers, and progress tracking.

2. How do I log my workouts?

Simply open the app, select an exercise, and input your sets, reps, and weights. You can also add custom exercises.

3. Does the app have a built-in exercise database?

Yes! The app includes a database of fundamental exercises with default weight recommendations and images to guide you.

4. Can I create my own exercises?

Absolutely! You can add custom exercises to personalize your training routine.

5. Does the app include a timer?

Yes, you can use the built-in timer to track rest periods between sets.

6. How can I track my progress?

The app features a workout calendar that logs your training history, allowing you to monitor your progress over time.

7. Is my workout data stored securely?

Yes, your data is stored securely and only accessible to you.
